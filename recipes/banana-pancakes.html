Banana Pancakes

About These Pancakes

These pancakes are thick, fluffy, and absolutely delicious! You'll be going back for seconds!

Making pancakes from scratch can seem daunting, but I promise you these are so easy you'll be making them all the time!

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe bananas
  • 2/3 cups almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 2 Tbls melted butter
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • .5 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. In a bowl mash the bananas and combine with the almond milk, vanilla extract, egg, and melted butter.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and pumpkin pie spice.
  3. Pour the mashed banana mixture into the flour mixture and mix by folding until just combined. DO NOT OVERMIX.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat. Once hot, add butter and batter. Cook a few minutes per side. It will vary between stoves.
  5. Remove from pan when fully cooked and serve immediately with syrup and your choice of toppings! Enjoy!
