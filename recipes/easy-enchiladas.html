Easy Enchiladas

About These Enchiladas

These enchiladas are an easy and quick week night meal.

These don't require a lot of ingredients and they won't break the bank.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups shredded chicken
  • 2 cups enchilada sauce
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2.5 cups Mexican blend cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • optional toppings: sour cream, lettuce, refired beans, etc.

Steps

  1. In a bowl combine shredded chicken and .5 cups of enchilada sauce.
  2. Fill the tortillas with the shredded chicken mixture and 1 cup of cheese. Roll them up and place seam side down in a baking dish.
  3. Pour remaining sauce and sprinkle remaining cheese over the top of the filled tortillas.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 F for 20 minutes or until cheese is metled and bubbly.
  5. Serve with any optional toppings. Enjoy!
